05 — Lab

The strange ideas go here first

Not everything here is finished. That's the point

Everyone — no single owner, by design

Every agency claims to be innovative. Few give innovation a room of its own, with no client, no deadline, and no guaranteed outcome. The Lab is that room.

It's where new tools get broken in before they touch client work, where the team's strangest ideas get a fair hearing, and where most experiments fail on purpose. The ones that survive end up changing how everything else here gets made.

Gandhi in 2024

AI × History

What would the most photographed man of his era look like in ours? A creative experiment placing Gandhi in the present day, built with generative imagery and a careful respect for the subject. Equal parts technology demo and question about how we remember.
